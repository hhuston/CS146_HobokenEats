Shake Shack

Price: $

Team Review:

Shake Shack is notorious for its tasty burger options, but they are pretty average across the board.

It is a good spot for a quick meal on the go, and the app allows you to get discounts from time to time.

The shakes and sides are the highlight of the meal, and are definitely recommended!
