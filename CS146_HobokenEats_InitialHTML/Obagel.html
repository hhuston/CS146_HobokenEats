O'Bagel

Price: $$

Rating: 5/5

Team Review:

O'Bagel is probably the best place in the city for fresh bagels and breakfast sandwiches.

Although a bit on the pricier side, it is the perfect takout spot for a tasty breakfast.

The quality is excellent, but if you hate waiting in a crowded store, make sure you order ahead!
