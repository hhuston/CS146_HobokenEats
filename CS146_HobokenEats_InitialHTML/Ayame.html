Ayame

Price: $$

Rating: 3/5

Team Review:

Ayame's interior allows for an impressive dining atmosphere, but it sushi leaves much to be desired.

Despite some flavorful signature rolls, the original and vegetarian options are not as top-notch.

The meals are priced fairly, and the hibachi dinner and specials are really tasty.
